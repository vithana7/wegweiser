⛺ Wild camping (Germany)

In Baden-Württemberg, wild camping is technically illegal but tolerated in emergencies and in Schutzhütten with a sleeping bag.

  • ✓ Stay in official shelters when possible
  • ✓ Leave no trace — pack out everything
  • ✗ No fires anywhere in the forest
  • ✓ Move on by morning

🦌 Wildlife on the trail

🐗 Wild Boar (Wildschwein)

Common, mostly avoid humans. Danger: mothers with piglets. Don't run — back away slowly, make yourself large.

🐍 European Adder (Kreuzotter)

Venomous. Active May–October on sunny rocky spots. Brown/grey with zigzag back. Shy — bites only if stepped on. Watch where you sit.

🦟 Ticks (Zecken)

Tiny. Hide in grass and low bushes up to ~1500m. Black Forest is a high-risk FSME zone. Full body check every evening — hairline, behind knees, armpits, waistline, groin.

🦌 Deer

Harmless and beautiful. Enjoy them.
